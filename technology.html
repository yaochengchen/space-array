Technology roadmap

From enabling technologies to an operational array.

The Space Array initiative connects science requirements to the technologies needed for formation-flying interferometry in space.

Relative navigation

GNSS, inter-satellite ranging, orbit determination, and baseline reconstruction.

Time and frequency

Clock stability, synchronization, phase transfer, and calibration architectures.

Receivers

Millimetre and sub-millimetre receiver technologies suitable for space operation.

Data transport

Optical links, onboard storage, compression, and downlink strategies.

Correlation

On-ground and distributed correlation architectures for long-baseline observations.

Imaging

Calibration, sparse arrays, dynamic imaging, and high-fidelity reconstruction.
